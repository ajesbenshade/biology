# Full Curriculum Map

## Unit 1: Scientific Method & Worldview
- Observation, hypothesis testing, controls, repeatability
- Data interpretation and claim evaluation
- Biblical worldview integration for scientific reasoning

## Unit 2: Chemistry of Life
- Atoms, bonding, water properties, macromolecules
- Enzymes, pH, energy transfer in cells

## Unit 3: Cells
- Prokaryotic vs. eukaryotic structure
- Membrane transport, cell cycle, mitosis/meiosis overview

## Unit 4: Genetics
- Inheritance patterns, molecular genetics, biotechnology
- Probability, chi-square, pedigree analysis
- [Unit 4 Daily Lessons]({{ "/lessons/unit4/" | relative_url }})

## Unit 5: Evolution and Changing Environments
- Adaptation and variation within kinds
- Fossils, molecular comparisons, environmental data
- Stewardship and resilience in changing ecosystems
- [Unit 5 Daily Lessons]({{ "/lessons/unit5/" | relative_url }})

## Unit 6: Ecology & Stewardship
- Population dynamics, food webs, nutrient cycles
- Human impact, conservation, and Christian stewardship
