# Teacher Guide: Unit 4 Day 9 – Human Genetics and Pedigrees

## Scripture Focus

**Philippians 2:4**

Frame family-based genetics with care, humility, and concern for real people.

## Teacher Objectives

- Teach pedigree symbols and pattern interpretation.
- Strengthen the use of multiple clues before naming an inheritance mode.
- Move students from quick guesses to supported explanations.

## Materials & Preparation

- Pedigree practice sets
- Symbol reference sheet
- Short writing frame for evidence-based conclusions

## Routine and Logistics Notes

- Start in pairs, then shift to small groups.
- Require two clues before teacher confirmation.
- Pull a short support table for overconfident sex-linked guesses.

## Bell Work Answer Key

Students should identify a likely clue in the pedigree rather than naming a pattern too quickly.

## Lesson Delivery Script (85 min)

1. **Bellwork (8 min):** Interpret a simple pedigree.
2. **Mini-Lesson (15 min):** Review symbols and common pedigree logic errors.
3. **Guided Practice Block 1 (25 min):** Analyze sample pedigrees in groups.
4. **Guided Practice Block 2 (25 min):** Write one evidence-based inheritance explanation.
5. **Debrief (10 min):** Share one clue that changed an initial conclusion.

## Exit Ticket Answer Key

Strong responses name one additional clue or data point that would increase confidence in a pedigree claim.

## Planning Note

Prepare to connect today's pedigree logic to tomorrow's probability work.

## Prayer Close

Ask for compassion and wisdom as students study genetics in human families.
