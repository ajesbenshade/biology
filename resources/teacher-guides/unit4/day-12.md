# Teacher Guide: Unit 4 Day 12 – Chapter 12 Review and Assessment

## Scripture Focus

**Proverbs 12:1**

Use the chapter close to frame correction as an essential part of wise learning.

## Teacher Objectives

- Close Chapter 12 with review and checkpoint evidence.
- Correct lingering heredity misconceptions before the biotechnology chapter begins.
- Use checkpoint evidence to identify the most important inheritance idea that still needs tightening.

## Materials & Preparation

- Chapter 12 review set or short checkpoint
- Correction tracker
- One or two high-frequency heredity error models

## Routine and Logistics Notes

- Review work stays independent before correction talk begins.
- Keep heredity materials active for the full period so the day remains a true chapter close.
- Focus corrections on one misconception at a time.

## Bell Work Answer Key

Accept one clear chapter strength and one specific heredity skill still needing attention.

## Lesson Delivery Script (85 min)

1. **Bellwork (8 min):** Students identify one Chapter 12 strength and one weak spot.
2. **Mini-Lesson (12 min):** Review two high-frequency heredity errors with corrected models.
3. **Guided Practice Block 1 (24 min):** Run review stations or a chapter checkpoint.
4. **Guided Practice Block 2 (26 min):** Correct one missed item immediately and revise one weak heredity explanation.
5. **Debrief (10 min):** Record one heredity idea now mastered and one skill still needing one final pass.

## Exit Ticket Answer Key

Strong responses name one heredity idea students can now explain clearly and one idea still needing a final correction.

## Planning Note

Treat this as a full Chapter 12 close rather than the start of Chapter 13, and use the checkpoint to tighten just one final heredity weakness per student.

## Prayer Close

Thank God for the ability to learn, review, accept correction, and prepare wisely for the next chapter.
