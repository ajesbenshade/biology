# Teacher Guide: Unit 4 Day 2 – DNA Structure and Replication

## Scripture Focus

**1 Corinthians 14:33**

Use the ordered structure of DNA as a reminder that confusion is reduced when students work carefully and sequentially.

## Teacher Objectives

- Teach DNA structure and complementary base pairing.
- Model semi-conservative replication clearly.
- Establish the class error-check routine for molecular work.

## Materials & Preparation

- DNA model pieces or diagrams
- Replication flowchart
- One flawed model for correction

## Routine and Logistics Notes

- Use the cue: check direction, check pairs, check enzymes.
- Keep partners on the same model for the full block.
- Pull a short reteach group for strand-direction confusion.

## Bell Work Answer Key

Students should sketch a double-stranded DNA segment with correct complementary base pairing.

## Lesson Delivery Script (85 min)

1. **Bellwork (8 min):** DNA sketch and partner check.
2. **Mini-Lesson (15 min):** Review DNA structure and walk through replication steps.
3. **Guided Practice Block 1 (25 min):** Build and label a DNA model.
4. **Guided Practice Block 2 (27 min):** Run the replication simulation and fix one flawed sample.
5. **Debrief (10 min):** Identify one fidelity safeguard and one common mistake.

## Exit Ticket Answer Key

Strong answers name a step such as correct complementary pairing or polymerase activity and explain its role in accuracy.

## Planning Note

Save the most common errors for the molecular workshop on Day 5.

## Prayer Close

Thank God for the reliability of biological information and the patience to study it well.
