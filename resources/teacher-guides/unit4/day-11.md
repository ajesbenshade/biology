# Teacher Guide: Unit 4 Day 11 – Chapter 12 Problem-Solving Clinic

## Scripture Focus

**Proverbs 15:31**

Present correction as something that leads to wisdom, not as something to avoid.

## Teacher Objectives

- Repair heredity errors before the chapter closes.
- Help students identify the first wrong step in a problem.
- Use targeted support inside Chapter 12 rather than after it.

## Materials & Preparation

- Grouped heredity clinic sets
- Readiness-check questions
- Error-pattern roster

## Routine and Logistics Notes

- Groups should be based on dominant error patterns.
- Students underline the first wrong step before revising.
- Keep the readiness check mostly quiet, then release one item for immediate correction.

## Bell Work Answer Key

Strong responses name where the sample answer first breaks down, not just how it ends.

## Lesson Delivery Script (85 min)

1. **Bellwork (8 min):** Diagnose one flawed heredity response.
2. **Mini-Lesson (12 min):** Review the most common chapter errors.
3. **Guided Practice Block 1 (30 min):** Run targeted heredity clinic groups.
4. **Guided Practice Block 2 (25 min):** Complete a short readiness check and immediate correction.
5. **Debrief (10 min):** Name one error pattern students now recognize faster.

## Exit Ticket Answer Key

Strong answers connect a corrected process move to improved heredity accuracy.

## Planning Note

Use clinic results to narrow the emphasis for Day 12 chapter close.

## Prayer Close

Ask for humility, perseverance, and wisdom in correction.
