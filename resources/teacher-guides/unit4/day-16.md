# Teacher Guide: Unit 4 Day 16 – Biotechnology Synthesis and Assessment Prep

## Scripture Focus

**James 1:5**

Invite students to seek wisdom calmly as they connect what they have learned and prepare to demonstrate it.

## Teacher Objectives

- Close Chapter 13 with a biotechnology synthesis lesson rather than a narrow practice-assessment block.
- Help students connect tools, data, and claims in one integrated response.
- End with one final Unit 4 review target for Day 17 preparation.

## Materials & Preparation

- Biotechnology synthesis packet
- One model synthesis response
- Short final-review tracker

## Routine and Logistics Notes

- Students write independently first, then revise with a partner.
- Short teacher conferences happen only after one full synthesis attempt.
- End each conference with one review target, not a long list.

## Bell Work Answer Key

Students should identify one strongest biotechnology strand and one final Unit 4 strand to review tonight.

## Lesson Delivery Script (85 min)

1. **Bellwork (8 min):** Quick self-check on strongest and weakest biotechnology strands.
2. **Mini-Lesson (12 min):** Model one full biotechnology synthesis task.
3. **Guided Practice Block 1 (28 min):** Complete the chapter synthesis packet.
4. **Guided Practice Block 2 (25 min):** Revise one synthesis response, complete a short cumulative check, and mark one review target.
5. **Closing Reflection (10 min):** Name the final focus for Day 17.

## Exit Ticket Answer Key

Strong responses identify one Chapter 13 idea now explained clearly and one final Unit 4 detail to review tonight.

## Planning Note

Carry the final-review tracker into Day 17 so support remains targeted, calm, and tied to one manageable next step.

## Prayer Close

Ask for peace, clarity, and steady preparation as students connect what they have learned and prepare for the summative assessment.
