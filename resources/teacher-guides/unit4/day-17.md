# Teacher Guide: Unit 4 Day 17 – Unit Assessment and Wrap-Up

## Scripture Focus

**Psalm 139:14**

Close the unit where it began: with gratitude for the design of living things and for growth in understanding.

## Teacher Objectives

- Administer the Unit 4 summative assessment fairly and consistently.
- Give students a short, meaningful reflection after the test.
- Bridge cleanly into Unit 5.

## Materials & Preparation

- Summative assessment copies
- Reflection slip
- Unit 5 preview note or slide

## Routine and Logistics Notes

- Set materials before students enter.
- Keep clarifications procedural only during testing.
- Do not begin reflection until all assessments are collected.

## Bell Work Answer Key

Students should write one reminder about how they will work carefully today.

## Lesson Delivery Script (85 min)

1. **Bellwork (5 min):** Quiet focus reminder.
2. **Assessment Directions (5 min):** Review timing and procedures.
3. **Summative Assessment (65 min):** Main testing block.
4. **Reflection (5 min):** Short written response after collection.
5. **Wrap-Up (5 min):** Brief Unit 5 transition.

## Exit Ticket Answer Key

Strong responses name one Unit 4 idea students can now explain better and one routine that supported that growth.

## Planning Note

Compare assessment patterns to Days 5, 11, and 16 notes to see where embedded chapter support helped most.

## Prayer Close

Thank God for growth, perseverance, and the chance to use knowledge with humility.
