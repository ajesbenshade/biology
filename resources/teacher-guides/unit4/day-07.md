# Teacher Guide: Unit 4 Day 7 – Mendelian Inheritance

## Scripture Focus

**James 3:17**

Frame accurate inheritance reasoning as orderly, careful, and open to correction.

## Teacher Objectives

- Launch Chapter 12 with monohybrid inheritance.
- Keep genotype and phenotype language precise.
- Build setup habits that support the full heredity chapter.

## Materials & Preparation

- Punnett square practice set
- Genotype-phenotype anchor chart
- One flawed sample response

## Routine and Logistics Notes

- First problem is always independent.
- Partner A places alleles first and Partner B checks box setup, then roles switch.
- Keep students from jumping straight to phenotype language.

## Bell Work Answer Key

Students should show a clean monohybrid setup and distinguish genotype from phenotype correctly.

## Lesson Delivery Script (85 min)

1. **Bellwork (8 min):** Solve a simple cross.
2. **Mini-Lesson (15 min):** Model genotype, phenotype, and monohybrid structure.
3. **Guided Practice Block 1 (27 min):** Complete Punnett square practice.
4. **Guided Practice Block 2 (25 min):** Compare genotype and phenotype predictions and revise one flawed answer.
5. **Debrief (10 min):** Record one setup habit worth keeping.

## Exit Ticket Answer Key

Strong responses name a setup step such as labeling parent alleles correctly before filling boxes.

## Planning Note

Save the most common setup errors for Day 11 clinic groups.

## Prayer Close

Ask for wisdom and patience as students learn to reason carefully about inheritance.
