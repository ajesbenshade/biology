# Teacher Guide: Unit 4 Day 10 – Inheritance Probability and Chi-Square

## Scripture Focus

**Luke 14:28**

Use calculation and planning as reminders that wise thinking often involves careful setup.

## Teacher Objectives

- Teach inheritance probability and chi-square reasoning.
- Keep students from overstating what the math proves.
- Prepare accurate quantitative habits for the chapter clinic.

## Materials & Preparation

- Probability set
- Chi-square practice page
- Calculator guidance or board model

## Routine and Logistics Notes

- Students show the setup line before calculating.
- Partner checks focus on per-category work before summing.
- Use a side table for interpretation support, not just arithmetic support.

## Bell Work Answer Key

Strong answers show probability setup clearly before giving a number.

## Lesson Delivery Script (85 min)

1. **Bellwork (8 min):** Solve a short probability question.
2. **Mini-Lesson (15 min):** Walk through expected and observed ratios plus the chi-square formula.
3. **Guided Practice Block 1 (27 min):** Complete probability and chi-square problems.
4. **Guided Practice Block 2 (25 min):** Revise one weak chi-square interpretation.
5. **Debrief (10 min):** Record one reliable quantitative habit.

## Exit Ticket Answer Key

Strong responses name a real chi-square error such as combining categories too early or overstating the conclusion.

## Planning Note

Use today's work to build Day 11 clinic groups by error type.

## Prayer Close

Thank God for minds that can reason carefully and describe patterns honestly.
