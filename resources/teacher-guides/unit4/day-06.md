# Teacher Guide: Unit 4 Day 6 – Chapter 11 Review and Assessment

## Scripture Focus

**Proverbs 12:1**

Use the chapter close to present assessment and correction as part of wise learning.

## Teacher Objectives

- Close Chapter 11 with a focused review and checkpoint.
- Correct lingering molecular misconceptions before heredity begins.
- Use results to plan the opening of Chapter 12.

## Materials & Preparation

- Short Chapter 11 checkpoint
- Review set
- Correction tracker

## Routine and Logistics Notes

- The checkpoint is independent before any partner correction begins.
- Immediate correction should focus on one missed item only.
- Keep the transition to heredity explicit at the end of class.

## Bell Work Answer Key

Accept one clear chapter strength and one specific area students still need to tighten.

## Lesson Delivery Script (85 min)

1. **Bellwork (8 min):** Students identify strongest and weakest Chapter 11 ideas.
2. **Mini-Lesson (12 min):** Review two high-frequency molecular errors.
3. **Guided Practice Block 1 (25 min):** Complete short chapter review in pairs.
4. **Guided Practice Block 2 (25 min):** Administer checkpoint and correct one missed item.
5. **Debrief (10 min):** Reflect on what students will carry into heredity.

## Exit Ticket Answer Key

Strong responses name one clear chapter concept students can now explain and one still needing review.

## Planning Note

Use results to sort which students need the most support early in the inheritance block.

## Prayer Close

Thank God for growth through assessment, correction, and steady learning.
