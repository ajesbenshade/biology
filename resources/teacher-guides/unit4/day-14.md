# Teacher Guide: Unit 4 Day 14 – Biotechnology Data Analysis

## Scripture Focus

**Proverbs 12:22**

Use careful data analysis to reinforce truthful explanation and restraint in scientific claims.

## Teacher Objectives

- Teach PCR and gel interpretation with control-based reasoning.
- Separate evidence from conclusion clearly.
- Prepare students for the biotechnology CER writing on Day 15.

## Materials & Preparation

- Gel images or data packets
- Control-lane model
- Weak versus strong explanation samples

## Routine and Logistics Notes

- Keep datasets closed until controls are labeled together.
- Partner checks begin with specific data points, not final claims.
- Pull a short group for students who ignore the negative control.

## Bell Work Answer Key

Strong answers identify why controls matter and which control most affects trust in the result.

## Lesson Delivery Script (85 min)

1. **Bellwork (8 min):** Short gel-control warm-up.
2. **Mini-Lesson (15 min):** Model a gel and separate evidence from interpretation.
3. **Guided Practice Block 1 (27 min):** Interpret two biotechnology datasets.
4. **Guided Practice Block 2 (25 min):** Revise one weak data explanation using better evidence.
5. **Debrief (10 min):** Record one detail that makes a data-based claim stronger.

## Exit Ticket Answer Key

Strong responses cite a specific band or control comparison and explain why it matters.

## Planning Note

Save strong evidence sentences for the Day 15 CER lesson.

## Prayer Close

Thank God for truth, clarity, and the discipline to say only what the data supports.
