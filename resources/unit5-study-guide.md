# Unit 5 Study Guide

## Key Topics:
- Evidence for evolution (fossils, anatomy, molecular biology).
- Natural selection and adaptation.
- Population genetics and speciation.
- Biodiversity and human impacts.

## Practice Questions:
1. Describe how homologous structures provide evidence for common design.
2. Explain the process of natural selection using an example.
3. Define genetic drift and give an example.
4. Discuss the role of stewardship in biodiversity conservation.
