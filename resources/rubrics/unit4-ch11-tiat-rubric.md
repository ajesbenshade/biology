# Chapter 11 TIAT — Expressing the Genetic Code

**Total Points:** 22 (12 core + 10 Biblical Stewardship & Worldview Integration)

| Category | Excelling (4 pts) | Succeeding (3 pts) | Approaching (2 pts) |
|---|---|---|---|
| Ask Questions | Identifies specific, relevant questions that drive the model and investigation. | Identifies clear questions related to the project goal. | Questions are incomplete or unclear. |
| Develop and Use a Model | Model clearly and accurately represents relationships among DNA, RNA, and protein expression. | Model is mostly accurate with minor gaps. | Model is incomplete or inaccurate. |
| Construct an Explanation | Uses model evidence to explain gene expression mechanisms and outcomes. | Uses some model evidence with partial explanation. | Explanation lacks evidence-based support. |

## Biblical Stewardship & Worldview Integration (10 pts)

- **Excelling (10–9):** Explicitly connects gene regulation precision to Psalm 139:13-16 and/or Colossians 1:16-17 with clear scientific evidence.
- **Succeeding (8–7):** Makes a clear Scripture-science connection with minor gaps in evidence or clarity.
- **Approaching (6–0):** Minimal or unclear worldview integration; weak evidence connection.
