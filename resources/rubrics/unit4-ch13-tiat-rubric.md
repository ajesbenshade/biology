# Chapter 13 TIAT — Genetics to the Rescue!

**Total Points:** 22 (12 core + 10 Biblical Stewardship & Worldview Integration)

| Category | Excelling (4 pts) | Succeeding (3 pts) | Approaching (2 pts) |
|---|---|---|---|
| Define the Problem | Identifies complete and relevant criteria/constraints for the design challenge. | Identifies most key criteria/constraints. | Problem definition is incomplete. |
| Design Solutions | Produces more than two strong candidate solutions with clear rationale. | Produces two viable solutions with some rationale. | Produces one or underdeveloped solution. |
| Evaluate Solutions | Critically evaluates alternatives using evidence and trade-offs. | Evaluates alternatives with partial evidence. | Evaluation is limited or lacks evidence. |

## Biblical Stewardship & Worldview Integration (10 pts)

- **Excelling (10–9):** Includes ethical-biblical constraints (Genesis 1:28 stewardship; Proverbs 2:6 wisdom) and applies them to design decisions.
- **Succeeding (8–7):** Clear ethics/worldview integration with minor gaps.
- **Approaching (6–0):** Minimal biblical-ethical reflection or weak application.
