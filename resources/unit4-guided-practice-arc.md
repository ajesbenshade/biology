# Unit 4 Guided Practice Arc

Prepared for Dock Mennonite High School Freshman Biology with an academic-excellence focus.

## Scope
- Chapter 11 molecular genetics practice and review
- Chapter 12 inheritance modeling, pedigrees, and quantitative reasoning
- Chapter 13 applications, data analysis, and biotechnology writing
- Alignment to rigorous freshman-level genetics expectations

## Strengths Highlighted
- Clear non-negotiable content targets in molecular genetics, heredity, and biotechnology
- Data-forward skills such as chi-square, control analysis, and CER writing
- Practice embedded inside each chapter instead of delayed until the end of the unit

## Guided Practice Snapshot
- Chapter 11 sequence tracing, mutation analysis, and review
- Chapter 12 inheritance clinics, pedigree work, and chapter close
- Chapter 13 application analysis, data interpretation, and assessment preparation

## Instructional Strengths
- Evidence-based reasoning activities and clear support structures
- Repeated problem-solving routines inside each chapter block
- Continued focus on objective assessment criteria with time for correction and mastery
