# Unit 4 Guided Practice Arc

Prepared for Dock Mennonite High School Freshman Biology with an academic-excellence focus.

## Scope
- Chapter 12 review skills
- Biotechnology introduction and applications
- Distributed guided practice before the Unit 4 assessment

## Guided Practice Snapshot

- Continued focus on objective assessment criteria with time for correction and mastery
