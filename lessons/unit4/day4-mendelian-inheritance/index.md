# Day 4: Mendelian Inheritance

## Objectives

- Solve monohybrid Punnett square problems
- Distinguish genotype from phenotype
- Apply chi-square to compare expected and observed ratios

## Bell Work (10 min)

Predict offspring outcomes from a simple dominant/recessive cross.

## Lesson Flow (70 min)

1. Mendel review and key vocabulary
2. Punnett square guided practice
3. Chi-square setup and interpretation

## Classwork / Homework

- 10 monohybrid practice problems
