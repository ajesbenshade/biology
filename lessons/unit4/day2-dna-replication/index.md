# Day 2: DNA Structure and Replication

## Objectives

- Model the DNA double helix
- Explain semi-conservative replication
- Identify roles of helicase, primase, DNA polymerase, and ligase

## Bell Work (10 min)

Sketch and label a DNA strand from memory.

## Lesson Flow (70 min)

1. Quick review of Day 1 vocabulary
2. DNA model build and strand-pair practice
3. Replication walk-through with enzyme roles

## Classwork / Homework

- Replication flowchart
- 5 replication practice questions

## Assessment

- Exit ticket: Explain why replication fidelity matters for inheritance
