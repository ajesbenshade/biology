# Day 4: Mendelian Inheritance – Laws of Heredity Within Kinds

**Scripture Focus:** Genesis 1:21 – “According to their kinds.”

**Objectives:**
- Use Punnett squares for monohybrid crosses.
- Contrast observable inheritance with molecules-to-man claims.

**Materials:**
- Punnett square worksheets.
- Coin-flip simulation.

**Bell Work (10 min):**
Predict offspring of tall/short pea plants (simple example provided).

**Lesson (70 min):**
1. Mendel review via National Geographic.
2. Practice crosses.
3. Worldview: Variation within kinds shows God’s provision for diversity, not new kinds.

**Classwork/Homework:**
10 monohybrid problems.

**Assessment:**
Chapter 11 Quiz (last 20 min).
