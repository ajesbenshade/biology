# Day 12: Unit Assessment and Wrap-Up

## Objectives

- Demonstrate mastery of Unit 4 genetics targets.
- Solve and explain textbook-aligned problems accurately.
- Reflect on strengths and next steps for Unit 5 readiness.

## Key Terms

- summative assessment
- justification
- evidence-based explanation
- proficiency
- reflection

## Textbook Content

- Unit 4 cumulative targets spanning Chapters 11–13.
- Final emphasis on clear reasoning and accurate scientific communication.

## In-Class Activities / Minilabs

1. **Bellwork (5 min):** Quick readiness routine and assessment expectations.
2. **Summative Assessment (60–90 min, schedule-dependent):** Unit 4 test/quiz.
3. **Wrap-Up Discussion:** “How does genetics reveal God’s glory?”
4. **Reflection (10 min):** Identify strongest skill and one area for growth.
5. **Preview (5 min):** Unit 5 bridge and expectations.

## Critical Perspective

- How do we distinguish confidence from demonstrated mastery?
- What evidence from your work best supports your self-assessment?

## Biblical Integration

Responsible scholarship includes honest evaluation of progress and gratitude for growth in knowledge and discipline.

## Homework / Assignment

- Optional correction set for retention and review.
- Prepare materials for Unit 5 launch.

## Exit Ticket / Reflection

What is one Unit 4 concept you can now explain clearly with evidence?

## Teacher Notes Only (not for students)

- Capture post-assessment trends to inform targeted reteaching.
- Maintain strictly academic scoring criteria.
