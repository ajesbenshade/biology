# Day 6: Human Genetics and Pedigrees

## Objectives

- Construct and analyze pedigree charts
- Distinguish autosomal and sex-linked inheritance clues
- Calculate probability for pedigree scenarios

## Bell Work (10 min)

Interpret one simple pedigree and identify likely carriers.

## Lesson Flow (70 min)

1. Pedigree symbol review
2. Inheritance pattern analysis in small groups
3. Probability practice from family data

## Classwork / Homework

- Pedigree analysis worksheet

## Assessment

- Short pedigree interpretation check
