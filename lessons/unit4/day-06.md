# Day 6: Human Genetics & Pedigrees

**Scripture Focus:** Acts 17:26 – “From one man he made all the nations.”

**Objectives:**
- Construct and analyze pedigrees.
- Discuss genetic disorders as a result of sin’s curse (Romans 8:22).

**Materials:**
- Pedigree worksheets.
- National Geographic case study.

**Bell Work (10 min):**
Analyze a simple pedigree chart provided.

**Lesson (70 min):**
1. Pedigree construction.
2. Group activity on disorders.
3. Biblical tie: All humans share common ancestry in Adam and Eve.

**Classwork/Homework:**
Short research summary on one disorder.
