Welcome to My Classroom!

Biology Teacher — Dock Mennonite High School

Hello, students and families! I am so excited to have you in Biology this year. This course will take us on an incredible journey through the science of life — from the tiniest cells to entire ecosystems. My goal is to make science engaging, relevant, and even a little surprising!

In this class you will ask big questions, conduct hands-on investigations, and develop skills that will serve you well in whatever path you choose after high school. I believe every student can succeed in science, and I am here to support you every step of the way.

Please don't hesitate to reach out with questions — my door (and inbox) is always open.

About Me

Background & Education

I have a Bachelor of Science in Biology and a Master of Arts in Teaching. Before coming to Dock Mennonite, I spent time working in field ecology and environmental education, which means I bring real-world science experience into our classroom every day.

Teaching Philosophy

I believe learning happens best through curiosity, collaboration, and hands-on exploration. In my classroom, mistakes are celebrated as part of the learning process, and questions are always welcome.
